I'm Morgan — an M.A. Design student at NTUST in HCI and Service Design. I'm the kind of designer who gets excited talking to users, then disappears into a prototype until it actually works.

I move between research, systems thinking, and building: mapping messy service ecosystems, turning insights into interaction logic, and shipping high-fidelity prototypes with Raspberry Pi, Firebase, GenAI, and Cursor.

From leading a 40-person service camp to workshops at Politecnico di Milano, I've learned to bring energy across cultures — and bring those perspectives back to local problems.

Three projects, three platforms, one belief: design can make difficult moments easier to understand and move through.
